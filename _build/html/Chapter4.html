4.2 Testing¶

Every system should undergo testing to ensure it meets the requirements specified and the needs of its users. The system should be tested to demonstrate that they do what they are intended to do, and they do not do what they are not supposed to do. A test plan should be developed before the actual testing begins. The basic strategies that were used for testing were following
